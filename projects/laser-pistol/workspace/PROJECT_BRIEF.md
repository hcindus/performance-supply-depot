## üìã PHASE 1: COMPONENT RESEARCH (This Week)

### Core Components Needed:

**1. Laser Diode**
- Power: 1W‚Äì5W (class 4, requires safety interlocks)
- Wavelength: 445nm (blue) or 638nm (red) ‚Äî visible, energetic
- Source: DTR's Laser Shop, eBay (trusted sellers), LaserTree

**2. Power Supply**
- Type: LiPo battery pack (11.1V or 14.8V)
- Capacity: 2000mAh+ for reasonable runtime
- Source: HobbyKing, Amazon, local RC shops

**3. Driver Circuit**
- Constant current driver for laser diode
- Adjustable power levels
- Safety shutoff feature
- Source: Custom build or LaserTree drivers

**4. Housing/Chassis**
- Material: Aluminum (heat dissipation + strength)
- Design: Pistol form factor
- Source: 80% lower (incomplete) + custom machining OR 3D printed + aluminum barrel

**5. Optics**
- Collimating lens (focus beam)
- Beam expander (optional, for range)
- Source: Edmund Optics, ThorLabs, eBay

**6. Safety Systems**
- Key switch (prevents unauthorized use)
- Dead man's switch (must hold to fire)
- Safety goggles (mandatory for operation)
- Source: Amazon, laser safety suppliers

**7. Cooling**
- Heat sink for laser diode
- Thermal paste
- Optional: small fan for high-power builds
