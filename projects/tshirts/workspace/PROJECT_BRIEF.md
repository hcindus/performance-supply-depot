## üè≠ PRODUCTION OPTIONS

### Option A: Print-on-Demand (POD)
**Suppliers:** Printful, Printify, TeePublic, Redbubble  
**Pros:** No inventory, ship direct, easy testing  
**Cons:** Higher per-unit cost, less quality control  
**Best for:** Initial prototypes, small batches  

### Option B: Screen Printing Shop (Local)
**Find:** Local screen printers  
**Pros:** Higher quality, bulk discounts, fast turnaround  
**Cons:** Minimum order quantities (usually 12‚Äì24)  
**Best for:** Bulk orders, consistent designs  

### Option C: DTG (Direct-to-Garment) Specialist
**Suppliers:** Custom DTG shops  
**Pros:** Full color, complex designs, no minimums  
**Cons:** Slightly higher cost, slower than screen print  
**Best for:** BR-01 art (full color galaxy prints)
