## 1. Introduction

Performance Supply Depot LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates Dusty, a non-custodial cryptocurrency dust consolidation service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

**IMPORTANT:** Dusty is a non-custodial service. We do not hold, store, or control your cryptocurrency private keys or funds. All transactions are executed directly on your behalf through your connected exchange APIs.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** Email address, username, and authentication credentials
- **API Credentials:** Exchange API keys (encrypted at rest, never logged in plaintext)
- **Communication Data:** Messages, support requests, and feedback you submit
- **Identity Verification:** If required for compliance, government-issued ID and proof of address

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Feature usage, session duration, error logs, performance metrics
- **Transaction Metadata:** Timestamps, transaction hashes, exchange endpoints (NOT amounts or balances)
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** Session management, security, analytics

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Exchange Data:** Account verification status, trading permissions (via API)
- **Blockchain Data:** Public transaction data from blockchain explorers
- **Compliance Partners:** Sanctions screening, identity verification results

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

### 5.1 Encryption

- **In Transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.3 encryption
- **At Rest:** Sensitive data, including API keys, is encrypted using AES-256

### 5.2 Access Controls

- Multi-factor authentication required for all administrative access
- Role-based access control limiting data exposure to necessary personnel
- Regular access reviews and revocation procedures

### 5.3 Security Monitoring

- Continuous security monitoring and intrusion detection
- Regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
- Incident response procedures with defined escalation paths

### 5.4 Non-Custodial Architecture

**Critical Security Feature:** Dusty does not hold your cryptocurrency private keys or funds. Your API keys are used only to execute transactions on your behalf through supported exchanges. This non-custodial design significantly reduces the risk of catastrophic fund loss.
