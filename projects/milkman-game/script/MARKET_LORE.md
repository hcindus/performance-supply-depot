## üéÆ GAME MECHANIC IDEAS FROM LORE

### Cream Addiction Mechanic
- Citizens become hypnotized (purple aura)
- Milk Man must cleanse with mist
- Failed cleanse = citizens attack player
- Successful cleanse = citizens join as allies

### Vil's Weakness
- When Shoezet appears, Vil stops attacking
- Dialogue boxes show his hesitation
- Player can exploit this to deal extra damage

### The Tainted Cream
- Vil sabotages vat during battle
- Changes level 3 dynamics
- Citizens become enraged instead of hypnotized
- Harder enemies but allies fight back

### Boss Battle: Shoezet Syndicate
- Children swarm in waves
- Shohezet commands from elevated position
- Cream vats explode creating hazard zones
- Player must platform to reach her
