# EPISODE 4: UDDER DESTRUCTION!
## Milk Man Live Action - Season 1, Episode 4

**Runtime:** 30 minutes  
**Genre:** Retro Sci-Fi Superhero  
**Tone:** Cheesy, heroic, family-friendly  

---

## COLD OPEN

### EXT. DAIRY FARMS - DAY

Rolling green hills. Cows graze peacefully. MILK FARMS dot the landscape.

**M.U.U. (V.O.)**  
*(narrating)*  
Dairyopolis's farms. Where fresh milk begins its journey.

### INT. COW BARN - DAY

A FARMER milks a COW. The milk flows into a bucket.

**FARMER**  
That's a good girl. Best milk in the county.

Then — the COW'S UDDER begins to GLOW.

**FARMER (CONT'D)**  
Whoa! What's wrong with you?

The udder GLOWS BRIGHTER. The cow MOOS in PANIC.

Then — the UDDER EXPLODES.

Milk SPRAYS everywhere. The cow COLLAPSES.

**FARMER (CONT'D)**  
MY COW! WHAT'S HAPPENING?!

---

## ACT ONE: THE COW ATTACKS

### EXT. MULTIPLE FARMS - DAY

Across Dairyopolis's farm region: COWS are EXPLODING. Their UDDERS glowing, then bursting.

**M.U.U. (V.O.)**  
*(alarmed)*  
Multiple bovine incidents detected. Dairy cows... attacking.

### EXT. FARM ROAD - CONTINUOUS

A COW walks down the road. Its udder is MASSIVE and GLOWING.

**CITIZEN**  
RUN! IT'S A MAD COW!

Citizens RUN. The cow CHASES, spraying MILK from its overfilled udder.

---

### INT. THE CREAMERY - CONTROL ROOM - DAY

Milk Man and Boy Lactate watch the SCREENS.

**MILK MAN**  
Cows? Attacking?

**M.U.U.**  
The bovine population appears to have developed hostile tendencies. I'm detecting extreme hormone fluctuations.

**BOY LACTATE**  
I thought cows were supposed to be gentle!

**M.U.U.**  
Normally, yes. Something is causing aggression.

A SCREEN shows a map with RED DOTS — farm after farm going red.

**M.U.U. (CONT'D)**  
Twelve farms compromised. Thirty-seven cows on rampage.

**MILK MAN**  
This is bad. Without cows... no milk.

---

## ACT TWO: THE SOURCE

### EXT. ABANDONED DAIRY PLANT - DAY

A dark, abandoned building. Inside: equipment covered in dust.

**DR. MASTITIS** emerges from shadows. A mad scientist in a white lab coat, with wild eyes and cow-themed devices.

**DR. MASTITIS**  
*(cackling)*  
Cows will be VICTORIOUS! The UDDER REVOLUTION has begun!

He's been injecting cows with his UDDER EXPANSION SERUM.

**DR. MASTITIS (CONT'D)**  
Once all cows are transformed... Dairyopolis will BEG for mercy!

---

### EXT. CITY STREETS - DAY

Milk Man and Boy Lactate run through chaos:

- Cows blocking traffic
- Milk spraying everywhere
- Citizens hiding

**MILK MAN**  
We need to find whoever's doing this!

**BOY LACTATE**  
But there are so many cows!

**M.U.U. (V.O.)**  
I've traced the source. An abandoned dairy plant in the farm district.

**MILK MAN**  
Let's go!

---

### EXT. ABANDONED DAIRY PLANT - DAY

The heroes arrive. Dr. Mastitis stands on the roof, administering serum to more cows.

**MILK MAN**  
HEY! What's going on here?!

**DR. MASTITIS**  
Milk Man! You've come to witness the UDDER REVOLUTION!

**MILK MAN**  
Udder revolution? That's ridiculous!

**DR. MASTITIS**  
RIDICULOUS?! Cows have been oppressed for THOUSANDSANDS of years! Milked against their will! Now it's THEIR TURN!

**BOY LACTATE**  
*(whispering)*  
This guy's crazy!

---

## ACT THREE: THE BATTLE

### ACTION SEQUENCE

**DR. MASTITIS** presses a BUTTON. A dozen COWS CHARGE from the building.

**MILK MAN**  
Scatter!

They RUN in different directions.

Milk Man DODGES charging cows:

- Jumps over a cow
- Slides under another
- Uses Lasso to swing up to a roof

**BOY LACTATE** throws CALCIUM TABLETS:

- Creates a BARRIER
- Stuns a cow temporarily
- Hits Dr. Mastitis's device, causing sparks

**DR. MASTITIS**  
You'll never stop the COW UPRISING!

**MILK MAN**  
It's not an uprising — it's a public health hazard!

---

### ROOFTOP FIGHT

Milk Man climbs to the roof. Dr. Mastitis tries to escape in a COW-POWERED CART.

**MILK MAN**  
Not so fast!

He uses the LACTOSE LASSO, SNARES the cart.

**DR. MASTITIS**  
NO!

The cart STOPS. Dr. Mastitis tumbles out.

**MILK MAN**  
You're under arrest, Doctor.

**DR. MASTITIS**  
You'll regret this! The cows will REMEMBER!

**MILK MAN**  
I'm sure they will.

---

## ACT FOUR: THE CURE

### INT. THE CREAMERY - LAB - DAY

Scientists work on a CURE for the cow serum.

**M.U.U.**  
We've developed an anti-serum. It should reverse the effects.

**MILK MAN**  
Deploy it!

### EXT. FARMS - DAY

The cure is sprayed from HELICOPTERS. Cows calms down. Udders return to normal.

**FARMER**  
*(relief)*  
My girls are back to normal! Thank you, Milk Man!

---

## EPILOGUE

### INT. THE CREAMERY - CONTROL ROOM - DAY

**M.U.U.**  
All cows have been stabilized. Dairy production resuming.

**BOY LACTATE**  
So... cows are okay now?

**MILK MAN**  
Back to normal. The Doctor was just... passionate about the wrong things.

**BOY LACTATE**  
You think cows have feelings?

**MILK MAN**  
I think everyone deserves fair treatment. Even cows.

---

## POST-CREDITS

### INT. LAB - NIGHT

Dr. Mastitis sits in a cell. A SHADOW appears.

**SHADOW**  
You failed.

**DR. MASTITIS**  
The cows were my masterpiece!

**SHADOW**  
Your "masterpiece" got you arrested. But I have use for someone with your... talents.

A DEVICE is passed through the bars: the BUTTER INJECTOR.

**SHADOW (CONT'D)**  
Let's see how the heroes handle BUTTER.
