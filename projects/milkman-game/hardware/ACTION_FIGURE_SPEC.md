## ğŸ”§ PRODUCTION NOTES

### Chassis Adaptation Strategy

**From CYLON-PRIME (48 parts) â†’ Action Figure:**
1. **Keep 35 parts** unchanged (joints, internal structure)
2. **Modify 10 parts** (head, torso, add character details)
3. **Add 11 new parts** (accessories, weapons, bases)
4. **Design 3 alternate accessory sets**

**Print Strategy:**
- **Batch 1:** Structural parts (joints, limbs) - PETG
- **Batch 2:** Detail parts (heads, accessories) - PLA
- **Batch 3:** Flexible parts (hands, some joints) - TPU

### Assembly Time Estimate
- **Printing:** ~120 hours per figure (all parts)
- **Cleanup:** 4 hours (supports, smoothing)
- **Assembly:** 2 hours (fitting, testing joints)
- **Painting:** 8 hours (detail work, weathering)
- **Electronics:** 1 hour (LED wiring)
- **TOTAL:** ~135 hours per figure

### Cost Per Figure
- **Filament:** ~$35 (PETG, PLA, specialty colors)
- **Magnets:** $5 (cape attachment)
- **LEDs:** $8 (bottles, eyes)
- **Batteries:** $3 (CR2032 x3)
- **Fabric:** $5 (cape, shawl)
- **Paint:** $10 (acrylics, glow paint)
- **TOTAL:** ~$66 per figure

### Suggested Settings
- **Layer height:** 0.2mm (0.12mm for faces)
- **Infill:** 15% standard, 25% joints
- **Walls:** 3 perimeters
- **Supports:** Tree supports, everywhere for overhangs
- **Print temperature:** PETG 240Â°C, PLA 200Â°C

### Critical Parts (High Detail)
- Faces: 0.12mm layer, 0% infill (solid)
- Hands: 0.2mm layer, TPU recommended
- Weapons: 0.2mm layer, print flat for strength
- Logo emblems: 0.12mm layer, MM or S detail
