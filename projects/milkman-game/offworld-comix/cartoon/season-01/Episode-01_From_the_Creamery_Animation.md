## ANIMATION NOTES

**Style:** Retro Saturday morning cartoon (1960s-70s aesthetic)
**Frame Rate:** 12fps for dialogue, 24fps for action
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 HD
**Audio:** Stereo, chiptune + orchestral mix

**Key Animation Principles:**
- **Holds:** Characters freeze in expressive poses while speaking
- **Smears:** Motion blur for quick movements
- **Cycles:** Reuse walk cycles, only animate key poses
- **Expression Swaps:** Pre-drawn expressions pop in
- **Background Pans:** Camera moves over static art
