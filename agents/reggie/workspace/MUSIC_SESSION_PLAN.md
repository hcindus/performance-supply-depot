### Phase 2: What Is Music? (10 minutes)

**Teach Basics:**

**Mylonen asked:** "What is music?"

**Answer:**
> "Music is organized sound that makes you feel things. It's patterns of pitch (high vs low), rhythm (fast vs slow), and timbre (what makes a piano sound different from a flute). But really... it's emotion you can hear."

**Elements:**
1. **Melody** ‚Äî The main tune you hum
2. **Harmony** ‚Äî Background support
3. **Rhythm** ‚Äî The beat, the pulse
4. **Timbre** ‚Äî The "voice" of the instrument
5. **Dynamics** ‚Äî Loud vs soft
6. **Texture** ‚Äî Thick (orchestra) vs thin (solo)

**Connect to Art:**
- Tappy: Color, shape, composition
- Reggie: Pitch, rhythm, timbre
- Both: Create emotion through pattern
