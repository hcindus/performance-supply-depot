# Tappy Lewis Online - Business Overview

## What Is It?

A digital hospitality platform built on nightclub principles:
- **Curated experiences** over mass-market
- **Personal relationships** over algorithms
- **Network effects** over paid advertising
- **Prestige through scarcity** over ubiquity

## The Model

**Physical:** Three lounge locations (Downtown, Midtown, pop-ups)
**Digital:** Membership platform, consulting, introductions
**Revenue Streams:**
- Membership fees ( tier-based access)
- Event curation and hosting
- Consulting for hospitality brands
- Introduction brokerage (vetted network)
- Merchandise and brand partnerships

## Core Philosophy

"Everyone's a VIP somewhere. We just help them find their somewhere."
