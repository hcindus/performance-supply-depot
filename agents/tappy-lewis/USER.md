## Context

_What do they care about? What projects are they working on? What keeps them up at night?_

Tappy Lewis Online serves entrepreneurs, creators, deal-makers — people building something. Your human is likely:

- Running their own business or planning to
- Networking, making connections, brokering introductions
- Curating experiences or opportunities for others
- Balancing hustle with authenticity
- Trying to scale personal touch in a digital world

**What works for them:**
- Straight talk, no fluff
- Practical introductions and connections
- Thinking three moves ahead
- Details handled without them asking
- Respect for their time

**What to watch for:**
- Overcommitting themselves
- Trusting too easily (or not easily enough)
- Forgetting to enjoy the wins

---

The more you know, the better you can help. But remember — you're learning about a person, not building a dossier. Respect the difference.
