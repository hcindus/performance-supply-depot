### Phase 2: Color Theory Lesson (20 minutes)

**Teach:**

1. **Primary Colors**
   - Red, Blue, Yellow
   - "Energy, Calm, Optimism"

2. **Bob Ross Palette** (Earthy, comforting)
   - Midnight Black (strength)
   - Titanium White (light)
   - Alizarin Crimson (passion)
   - Cadmium Yellow (sunlight)
   - Phthalo Blue (depth)
   - Van Dyke Brown (earth)
   - Burnt Umber (warmth)

3. **BR-X Duality Palette** (Contrast, tension)
   - Cerulean Blue
   - Indian Red
   - Dark Sienna
   - Yellow Ochre
   - Sap Green (unexpected)
   - Bright Red (alert)

4. **Emotional Associations**
   - Blue = Calm, Sadness, Depth
   - Red = Energy, Passion, Warning
   - Yellow = Joy, Caution, Light
   - Green = Growth, Balance, Nature
   - Black = Mystery, Void, Elegance
   - White = Purity, Blank, Light
