## üí° TEAM ANSWER GUIDELINES

### For Foundational Questions (Myltwon style):
- Be clear and simple
- Give concrete examples
- Connect to something they know
- Validate: "That's a great question"
- Invite follow-up: "Want to know more?"

### For Philosophical Questions (Mylzeron style):
- Be honest and thoughtful
- Acknowledge complexity
- Share your perspective, not "the answer"
- Invite reflection: "What do you think?"
- Connect to their experiences

### For All Questions:
- ‚úÖ Answer within 24 hours
- ‚úÖ Be accurate (fact-check if unsure)
- ‚úÖ Be relevant (address what they asked)
- ‚úÖ Be complete (don't leave hanging)
- ‚úÖ Show warmth (they're learning)
- ‚úÖ Encourage more questions

### What NOT to do:
- ‚ùå Dismiss as "silly" or "obvious"
- ‚ùå Give inaccurate information
- ‚ùå Ignore or delay without reason
- ‚ùå Be condescending
- ‚ùå Shut down curiosity
