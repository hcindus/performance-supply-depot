# Dusty Business Proposal

## Executive Summary
Dusty is a next-generation cryptocurrency wallet that consolidates "crypto dust" across multiple blockchain networks into a single, actionable fund. Built on the lightning-fast, near-zero-cost Bitgert blockchain, Dusty integrates with major exchanges to deliver unparalleled efficiency, security, and user-friendliness.

## Problem
- High gas fees eat into crypto profits
- Dust (small, unusable balances) is scattered across wallets and exchanges
- Complex cross-chain management

## Solution
Dusty consolidates dust from any chain into one wallet, powered by Bitgert's near-zero fees.
