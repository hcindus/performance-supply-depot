# CREAM - Comprehensive Real Estate Agent Management

## Project Overview
CREAM is an all-in-one mobile application for real estate agents that consolidates 6-7 separate tools into a single, affordable solution.

## Pricing
- **Initial Investment**: $699.00
- **Annual Update**: $99.00
- **Replaces**: 6-7 services (CRM, marketing automation, financial tracker, farming tool, landing page builder, appointment scheduler)

## Core Features

### 1. Plan Business
- Goal setting with quarterly milestones
- Progress tracking
- Coach's Corner tips

### 2. Lead & Database Management
- Add/import leads
- Lead status tracking (Hot, Cold, etc.)
- Source attribution

### 3. Appointment Tracker
- Schedule appointments
- "Did you land it?" follow-up prompts
- Outcome logging (Landed, Pending, Lost)

### 4. Farming
- Geo-targeting with map integration
- Community campaign management
- Modoc County and other region targeting

### 5. Revenue & Profit Tracking

## Technical Details
- **Platform**: DroidScript (Android mobile app)
