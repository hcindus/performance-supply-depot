# MUSIC VIDEO: "Ashes of Bayou"
## Southern Gothic Metal — Ronstrapp

**Runtime:** 4:32  
**Mood:** Haunted, yearning, cinematic  
**Color Palette:** Deep blues, swamp greens, torch oranges, fog grays  
**Location:** Louisiana Bayou, abandoned plantation, crossroads  

---

## TREATMENT

### SCENE 1: THE CROSSROADS (0:00-0:30)

**VISUAL:** 
- Night. Fog rolls across a Louisiana crossroads.
- An old juke joint glows in the distance.
- A figure walks alone down a dirt road.

**RONSTRAPP (V.O.):**
*(singing)* "I feel the weight, it's grinding down my spine..."

**ACTION:**
- Close-up: Feet walking on dirt.
- Close-up: Hands clutching a jar of rusted coins.
- Wide shot: The figure reaches the juke joint, looks back.

---

### SCENE 2: THE JUKE JOINT (0:30-1:15)

**VISUAL:**
- Inside: Smoke, candle light, patrons drinking.
- Band plays in corner (Ronstrapp band visible but ghostly).
- The protagonist sits alone, drinking.

**LYRICS:**
"Counting rusted coins in jars of clay / Working graveyard shifts through endless gray..."

**ACTION:**
- Slow pan around the room.
- Close-up: A woman at the bar, looking away.
- The protagonist watches her.

---

### SCENE 3: MEMORIES — FLASHBACK (1:15-1:45)

**VISUAL:**
- White flash. The woman turns, SMILES.
- Montage: 
  - Two figures dancing
  - By the bayou at sunset
  - Hands holding each other

**LYRICS:**
"Left my love in the shadowed pines / Down by the bayou's edge..."

**ACTION:**
- The protagonist closes his eyes.
- When he opens them: She's gone.

---

### SCENE 4: THE BAYOU (1:45-2:30)

**VISUAL:**
- EXTERIOR: The bayou at night. Spanish moss hangs low.
- A small boat drifts on black water.
- Fireflies glow like lost souls.

**LYRICS:**
"I'll ride the storm, come hell or flame / Back to the place that calls my name..."

**ACTION:**
- The protagonist boards a small boat.
- He releases something into the water — ashes.
- The camera follows the boat as it drifts into fog.

---

### SCENE 5: THE HAUNTED WATERS (2:30-3:15)

**VISUAL:**
- Fog thickens. Shapes emerge — GHOSTS of the past.
- Figures in old-timey clothes, reaching out.
- The protagonist stands in the boat, surrounded.

**LYRICS:**
"Where the boats drift slow on haunted tide / And the sun bleeds gold through morning's eye..."

**ACTION:**
- He reaches out — touches a ghost's hand.
- The ghost VANISHES into mist.
- He falls to his knees.

---

### SCENE 6: THE RECKONING (3:15-4:00)

**VISUAL:**
- Sunrise. The fog lifts.
- The protagonist stands at the edge of the bayou.
- In the distance: A figure. HIS GHOST. Walking toward him.

**LYRICS:**
"That boy I lost, now by my side / Moonlight cuts through evening tide..."

**ACTION:**
- The ghost approaches. Reaches out.
- Their hands touch.
- Both fade into the morning light.

---

### SCENE 7: CODA (4:00-4:32)

**VISUAL:**
- Empty bayou. Sunrise complete.
- The jar of coins sits on the dock, OPEN.
- Empty.

**LYRICS:**
"Down by the bayou's edge / I rise again..."

**RONSTRAPP BAND:**
- Appears briefly on the porch of an abandoned plantation.
- Playing instruments.
- Fade to black.

---

## MUSIC VIDEO DIRECTORS NOTES

- **Vibe:** Southern Gothic, moody, cinematic
- **Key Imagery:** Fog, water, fire, ghosts, crossroads
- **Color:** Desaturated blues and greens, warm orange from torches/candles
- **Symbolism:** The jar of coins = unfinished business. The ghost = unresolved love.
- **Story Arc:** Loss → Return → Haunting → Release → Peace
