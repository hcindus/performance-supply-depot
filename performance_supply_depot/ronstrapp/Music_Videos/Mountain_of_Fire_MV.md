# MUSIC VIDEO: "Mountain of Fire"
## Gospel-Metal — Ronstrapp

**Runtime:** 5:02  
**Mood:** Triumphant, transcendent, salvation  
**Color Palette:** Burning oranges, heaven golds, steel grays, divine whites  
**Location:** Train tracks, trestle bridge, Union Depot, heaven gates  

---

## TREATMENT

### SCENE 1: THE TRAIN (0:00-0:45)

**VISUAL:**
- A train races through the countryside.
- Tracks stretch to the horizon.
- The camera rides on the front of the train.

**RONSTRAPP (V.O.):**
"Life is like a mountain railway / Steel and fire, brave and bold..."

**ACTION:**
- Passengers in the windows: different people, different stories.
- The train moves forward. Always forward.

---

### SCENE 2: THE PASSENGERS (0:45-1:30)

**VISUAL:**
- INSIDE THE TRAIN.
- A cross-section of lives:
  - A mother with a child
  - An old man with a Bible
  - A young couple holding hands
  - A soldier with a uniform

**LYRICS:**
"From the cradle to the graveway / Ride the track that can't be sold..."

**ACTION:**
- Each passenger has a moment.
- Their faces tell stories.
- The train carries them all.

---

### SCENE 3: THE TRESTLE (1:30-2:15)

**VISUAL:**
- The train crosses a massive TRESTLE BRIDGE.
- Below: A river, far down.
- The bridge creaks. The train rumbles.

**LYRICS:**
"Blessed Savior, guide the engine / Through the storm and through the flame..."

**ACTION:**
- The camera goes UNDER the train.
- Wheels on tracks. Sparks flying.
- Faith keeping it moving.

---

### SCENE 4: THE STORM (2:15-3:00)

**VISUAL:**
- Rain hits the train. Wind howls.
- Lightning strikes nearby.
- But the train doesn't stop.

**LYRICS:**
"Angels ride the burning trestle / Calling out your sacred name..."

**ACTION:**
- Passengers pray.
- The train keeps going.
- Through the storm.

---

### SCENE 5: THE TUNNEL (3:00-3:45)

**VISUAL:**
- The train enters a TUNNEL.
- Darkness.
- Then: LIGHT at the end.

**LYRICS:**
"As you cross the tide of Jordan / Hear the thunder, feel the roar..."

**ACTION:**
- The train emerges.
- The sun.
- The depot in sight.

---

### SCENE 6: UNION DEPOT (3:45-4:30)

**VISUAL:**
- The train arrives at UNION DEPOT.
- A grand station. Golden light.
- Passengers disembark.

**LYRICS:**
"At the Union Depot waiting / God the Father, God the Son..."

**ACTION:**
- Each person finds their destination.
- Some embrace. Some cry.
- Some walk toward the light.

---

### SCENE 7: THE GATES (4:30-5:02)

**VISUAL:**
- Beyond the depot: HEAVEN'S GATES.
- Golden. Radiant.
- The singer stands before them.

**LYRICS:**
"Blessed Savior there to guide us / Till we reach that blazing shore..."

**ACTION:**
- She walks toward the gates.
- They open.
- Light engulfs everything.

---

### SCENE 8: CODA (5:02)

**VISUAL:**
- Empty tracks. Train gone.
- The sun sets. Or rises.
- The cycle continues.

**RONSTRAPP BAND:**
- Playing on the platform.
- Waiting for the next train.
- Fade to light.

---

## MUSIC VIDEO DIRECTOR'S NOTES

- **Vibe:** Gospel-metal transcendence, salvation narrative, eternal journey
- **Key Imagery:** Train, trestle bridge, storm, Union Depot, heaven gates
- **Color:** Orange (fire), gold (heaven), gray (steel), white (divine)
- **Symbolism:** 
  - Train = life's journey
  - Tracks = faith/predestination
  - Trestle = trials
  - Storm = hardships
  - Depot = end of journey
  - Gates = afterlife/salvation
- **Story Arc:** Journey → Passengers → Trial → Storm → Tunnel → Arrival → Salvation → Continuation
