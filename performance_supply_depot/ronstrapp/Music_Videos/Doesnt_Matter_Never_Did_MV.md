# MUSIC VIDEO: "Doesn't Matter, Never Did"
## Glam-Thrash — Ronstrapp

**Runtime:** 3:32  
**Mood:** Defiant, anthemic, breakup liberation  
**Color Palette:** Electric pinks, chrome silvers, neon purples, midnight blacks  
**Location:** Rock club stage, glitter explosion, city rooftops  

---

## TREATMENT

### SCENE 1: THE ROCK CLUB (0:00-0:30)

**VISUAL:**
- A rock club, packed. Smoke. Lights.
- The stage is empty.
- Then — SPOTLIGHT.

**RONSTRAPP (V.O.):**
"There you go, and baby—here I stand / You left me wrecked with a drink in my hand..."

**ACTION:**
- The lead singer walks onto stage.
- Guitar slung low.
- Grins.

---

### SCENE 2: THE BREAKUP MONTAGE (0:30-1:15)

**VISUAL:**
- QUICK CUTS of a relationship ending:
  - Throwing clothes out a window
  - Tearing up photos
  - Dumping drinks
  - Walking out doors

**LYRICS:**
"Golly gee? Nah, more like hellfire spree / But I guess it doesn't matter—never did to me..."

**ACTION:**
- She sings directly to camera.
- No sympathy. Just... DONE.

---

### SCENE 3: THE GLITTER EXPLOSION (1:15-2:00)

**VISUAL:**
- During the chorus: GLITTER EXPLODES from the stage.
- Covers the crowd.
- Everyone is sparkling.

**LYRICS:**
"No use crying, I'm done trying / Tore up the nights, set fire to the days..."

**ACTION:**
- She wades into the crowd.
- Everyone sings along.
- Hands up. Dancing.

---

### SCENE 4: THE BRIDGE (2:00-2:30)

**VISUAL:**
- ROOFTOP. City lights below.
- She stands at the edge.
- Wind in her hair.

**LYRICS:**
"You were a ghost in glitter / Now I'm dancing on your grave..."

**ACTION:**
- She spreads her arms.
- The city is hers.
- She doesn't look back.

---

### SCENE 5: THE ANTHEM (2:30-3:15)

**VISUAL:**
- BACK AT THE CLUB.
- FULL ENERGY.
- Band is destroying.
- Crowd is WILD.

**LYRICS:**
"You won't matter anymore / You never mattered before..."

**ACTION:**
- She's on the speakers.
- The crowd is bouncing.
- Pure liberation.

---

### SCENE 6: CODA (3:15-3:32)

**VISUAL:**
- After the show.
- Empty club.
- She sits on the stage. Drinking. Smiling.

**LYRICS:**
*(whispered)* "Never did..."

**RONSTRAPP BAND:**
- Packs up instruments.
- Walks out together.
- Fade to black.
