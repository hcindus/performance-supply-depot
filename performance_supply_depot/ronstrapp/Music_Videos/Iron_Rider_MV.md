# MUSIC VIDEO: "Iron Rider"
## Gothic-Western Metal — Ronstrapp

**Runtime:** 4:22  
**Mood:** Solitary, haunting, cinematic  
**Color Palette:** Rust browns, sunset oranges, steel grays, desert dust  
**Location:** Desert railroad, ghost town, moving train  

---

## TREATMENT

### SCENE 1: THE DESERT (0:00-0:45)

**VISUAL:**
- Vast desert at sunset. Red and orange sky.
- A figure on horseback in the distance.
- The camera watches, waiting.

**RONSTRAPP (V.O.):**
"Iron rider / Why won't you drop the mask?"

**ACTION:**
- Slow approach to the rider.
- He's cloaked. Shadowed face.
- He doesn't look back. He never does.

---

### SCENE 2: THE GHOST TOWN (0:45-1:30)

**VISUAL:**
- Abandoned Western town. Saloon doors swinging.
- Wooden buildings, weathered by sun and sand.
- The rider enters. Stops in the center.

**LYRICS:**
"You've been chasing shadows / Down rusted tracks..."

**ACTION:**
- He dismounts. Walks through the town.
- Opens doors. Looks inside.
- Nothing but dust and memories.

---

### SCENE 3: THE SALOON (1:30-2:15)

**VISUAL:**
- Inside the saloon: Broken tables, chairs overturned.
- A piano in the corner, keys missing.
- A deck of cards on the bar.

**LYRICS:**
"Don't draw the queen of blades, my friend / She'll cut you clean, she won't pretend..."

**ACTION:**
- He sits at the bar.
- Deals a hand of solitaire.
- Plays against himself. Always loses.

---

### SCENE 4: THE MEMORY (2:15-3:00)

**VISUAL:**
- The saloon TRANSFORMS.
- Ghostly figures appear: a woman, a man, a child.
- They're having a party. Laughing. Dancing.
- The rider watches.

**LYRICS:**
"You've had gold laid at your feet / But you chase ghosts you'll never meet..."

**ACTION:**
- He reaches for the woman.
- She vanishes.
- The party continues without him.

---

### SCENE 5: THE TRAIN (3:00-3:45)

**VISUAL:**
- EXTERIOR: A train in the distance.
- The tracks stretch forever.
- The rider stands on the tracks. Waiting.

**LYRICS:**
"And freedom? Just a tale they sell / Your prison is the road / You walk alone through hell..."

**ACTION:**
- The train APPROACHES. Loud. Thundering.
- He doesn't move.
- At the last second — he steps aside.

---

### SCENE 6: THE CHOICE (3:45-4:22)

**VISUAL:**
- The train passes. Gone.
- He's alone again.
- But now: a light on the horizon. A BUILDING.

**LYRICS:**
"Iron rider / Come down from your throne / Open the gate / You're not alone..."

**ACTION:**
- He looks at the light.
- Looks at his horse.
- Makes a choice: walks toward the light.

---

### SCENE 7: CODA (4:22)

**VISUAL:**
- The light: a farmhouse. Warm. Alive.
- A woman on the porch. Waving.
- He approaches.

**RONSTRAPP BAND:**
- Playing on the farmhouse porch.
- He joins them.
- Fade to sunset.

---

## MUSIC VIDEO DIRECTOR'S NOTES

- **Vibe:** Western Gothic, revisionist nostalgia, solitary redemption
- **Key Imagery:** Desert, train, ghost town, cards, porch light
- **Color:** Rust, orange, steel gray, warm yellow (for the light)
- **Symbolism:** 
  - Train = time/mortality
  - Cards = fate
  - Ghost town = past
  - Porch light = hope/redemption
- **Story Arc:** Riding → Arrival → Memory → Train → Choice → Redemption
