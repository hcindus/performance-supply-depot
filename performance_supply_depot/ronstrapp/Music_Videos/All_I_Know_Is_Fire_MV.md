# MUSIC VIDEO: "All I Know Is Fire"
## Symphonic Power-Metal — Ronstrapp

**Runtime:** 4:45  
**Mood:** Passionate, cinematic, volcanic  
**Color Palette:** Molten reds, gold embers, black obsidian, ash grays  
**Location:** Volcano interior, obsidian palace, lava rivers  

---

## TREATMENT

### SCENE 1: THE VOLCANO (0:00-0:45)

**VISUAL:**
- INSIDE A VOLCANO. Magma flows like rivers.
- The camera descends through rock tunnels.
- Heat waves distort the air.

**RONSTRAPP (V.O.):**
"Look at this face / The years carved deep..."

**ACTION:**
- The singer walks along a narrow obsidian ledge.
- Lava flows below. Sparks rise.
- She doesn't flinch. She walks with purpose.

---

### SCENE 2: THE MIRROR (0:45-1:30)

**VISUAL:**
- A pool of lava, still like a mirror.
- She looks down. Her reflection is... DIFFERENT.
- Older. Warmer. More alive.

**LYRICS:**
"Look at this life / Still chasing flame / No map, no crown, no name..."

**ACTION:**
- She touches the surface.
- Her reflection reaches back.
- They touch. Lava ripples.

---

### SCENE 3: THE MEMORY (1:30-2:15)

**VISUAL:**
- The lava DISSOLVES into a MEMORY:
  - A lover's face in the flames
  - Hands reaching across darkness
  - A kiss in the shadows

**LYRICS:**
"I don't know much / But I know I burn for you..."

**ACTION:**
- She dances with the memory-lover.
- Moves like fire. Flickering.
- Then — they merge. Become one.

---

### SCENE 4: THE ORDEAL (2:15-3:00)

**VISUAL:**
- Reality returns. The volcano QUAKES.
- Rocks fall. Lava rises.
- She must move.

**LYRICS:**
"I don't know much / But I know I burn for you..."

**ACTION:**
- She RUNS through the tunnels.
- Jumps across lava rivers.
- The volcano tries to consume her.

---

### SCENE 5: THE REVELATION (3:00-3:45)

**VISUAL:**
- She emerges into a CHAMBER.
- In the center: A PALACE made of obsidian.
- And a THRONE.

**LYRICS:**
"So many doors I never opened / So many truths I never spoke..."

**ACTION:**
- She climbs the palace steps.
- Sits on the throne.
- The volcano CALMS. Recognizes her.

---

### SCENE 6: THE CORONATION (3:45-4:22)

**VISUAL:**
- She sits on the throne. FIRE surrounds her.
- But it's not destructive. It's... HOME.
- The camera circles her.

**LYRICS:**
"But when you're near, the fog is broken / And I see the only truth I'll ever know..."

**ACTION:**
- She raises her hands.
- The fire RESPONDS.
- Becomes wings. A crown. A shield.

---

### SCENE 7: CODA (4:22-4:45)

**VISUAL:**
- Exterior: The volcano, smoking.
- But no eruption. Just... warmth.
- Smoke rises like a prayer.

**LYRICS:**
"I don't know much / But I know I burn for you..."

**RONSTRAPP BAND:**
- Appears on the volcano rim.
- Playing instruments.
- Fade to ember glow.

---

## MUSIC VIDEO DIRECTOR'S NOTES

- **Vibe:** Volcanic passion, symphonic grandeur, fire as love metaphor
- **Key Imagery:** Lava rivers, obsidian palace, fire wings, throne
- **Color:** Red, gold, black, ash gray
- **Symbolism:** 
  - Volcano = inner fire/passion
  - Obsidian = transformed pain
  - Throne = self-acceptance
  - Fire = love that destroys and redeems
- **Story Arc:** Descent → Mirror → Memory → Ordeal → Revelation → Coronation → Ascent
