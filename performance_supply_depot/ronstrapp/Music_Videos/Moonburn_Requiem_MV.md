# MUSIC VIDEO: "Moonburn Requiem"
## Bilingual Doom-Metal — Ronstrapp

**Runtime:** 5:15  
**Mood:** Melancholic, passionate, tragic  
**Color Palette:** Deep purples, silver moons, midnight blues, blood reds  
**Location:** Desert ruins, moonlit ocean, burning ballroom  

---

## TREATMENT

### SCENE 1: THE DESERT RUINS (0:00-0:45)

**VISUAL:**
- Night. Ancient stone ruins in a desert.
- A lone figure kneels in the sand.
- The moon dominates the sky — HUGE, silver, watching.

**RONSTRAPP (V.O.):**
*(Spanish)* "Lo siento, mi vida / Ya sé que terminó..."

**ACTION:**
- Close-up: The figure's hands dig into sand.
- Wide: She's burying something — a PHOTO.
- Wind blows. Sand covers the photo.

---

### SCENE 2: THE MEMORY (0:45-1:30)

**VISUAL:**
- FLASHBACK: A ballroom. Elegant. Couples dancing.
- She dances with a man — her lover.
- They smile. Happiness.

**LYRICS:**
"Cuando brille la luna / Sé que no dormirás..."

**ACTION:**
- Slow motion: Their hands touching.
- Close-up: Her eyes, full of love.
- Then — the scene FRACTURES. Glass breaks.

---

### SCENE 3: THE OCEAN (1:30-2:15)

**VISUAL:**
- Coastline at night. Waves crash against rocks.
- She walks along the edge, dress flowing.
- The ocean is dark, endless.

**LYRICS:**
*(English)* "The night you walked away / Tore my soul in two..."

**ACTION:**
- She screams into the wind.
- Waves crash behind her.
- The camera pulls back — she's so small against the vastness.

---

### SCENE 4: THE BURNING BALLROOM (2:15-3:00)

**VISUAL:**
- The ballroom — now ABLAZE. Fire everywhere.
- She stands in the doorway, watching it burn.
- The man is gone. Only smoke remains.

**LYRICS:**
"Left me with a hollow flame / And a sky that never knew..."

**ACTION:**
- She doesn't cry. She just watches.
- Fire reflects in her eyes.
- She turns, walks away. The ballroom collapses behind her.

---

### SCENE 5: THE MOUNTAIN (3:00-3:45)

**VISUAL:**
- She climbs a mountain at night.
- The moon is her companion.
- She reaches the peak. Looks down at the world below.

**LYRICS:**
"I'll see it through / Though the shadows scream..."

**ACTION:**
- Wind whips her hair.
- She raises her arms — like she's embracing the moon.
- The camera circles her, slowly.

---

### SCENE 6: THE RECKONING (3:45-4:30)

**VISUAL:**
- Back at the desert ruins. Daylight now.
- She stands in the same spot.
- But she's changed. Stronger. Harder.

**LYRICS:**
*(Spanish + English)* "I'll rise again / From the ash and flame..."

**ACTION:**
- She places a new stone on the ruins — a MEMORIAL.
- She touches it. Walks away.
- Doesn't look back.

---

### SCENE 7: CODA (4:30-5:15)

**VISUAL:**
- Final shot: The moon, rising again.
- Silver. Beautiful. Indifferent.

**LYRICS:**
*(Spanish, whispered)* "Lo siento, mi vida / Ya sé que terminó..."

**RONSTRAPP BAND:**
- Silhouetted on a cliff edge.
- Playing instruments.
- Fade to silver.

---

## MUSIC VIDEO DIRECTOR'S NOTES

- **Vibe:** Tragic romance, passionate intensity, cinematic tragedy
- **Key Imagery:** Moon, fire, ocean, ruins, ballroom
- **Color:** Silver/blue for moon, red/orange for fire, deep purple for grief
- **Symbolism:** 
  - Moon = witness to heartbreak
  - Ballroom = lost love
  - Fire = destruction of the past
  - Mountain = ascent from grief
- **Story Arc:** Loss → Memory → Despair → Destruction → Ascent → Release
- **Language:** Alternates Spanish/English to match song structure
