# MUSIC VIDEO: "The Story of My Knife"
## Glam-Goth Metal — Ronstrapp

**Runtime:** 4:55  
**Mood:** Noir, romantic, dangerous  
**Color Palette:** Neon blues, rain grays, blood reds, chrome silvers  
**Location:** Rain-soaked city, alleyways, late-night diner, neon signs  

---

## TREATMENT

### SCENE 1: THE ALLEYWAY (0:00-0:45)

**VISUAL:**
- Rain. Always rain.
- A neon sign flickers: "BAR."
- An alleyway. Steam rises from grates.

**RONSTRAPP (V.O.):**
"There's someone waiting / Alone in the rain / For a stranger to whisper their name..."

**ACTION:**
- The lead singer walks alone.
- Cigarette smoke in the cold air.
- She stops. Waits.

---

### SCENE 2: THE BAR (0:45-1:30)

**VISUAL:**
- Inside: Smoke, dim lights, empty stools.
- A bartender cleans a glass.
- She sits. Orders. Drinks alone.

**LYRICS:**
"Here you are / In the city of glass / Where love fades fast / And no one asks..."

**ACTION:**
- She watches the door.
- People come. Go.
- No one stops.

---

### SCENE 3: THE STRANGER (1:30-2:15)

**VISUAL:**
- A man enters. Sits beside her.
- They talk. Laugh.
- Something clicks.

**LYRICS:**
"Woman, sweet woman / Come home with me / You shine like fire / You move like free..."

**ACTION:**
- They leave together.
- Walk into the rain.
- The camera follows.

---

### SCENE 4: THE RELATIONSHIP MONTAGE (2:15-3:00)

**VISUAL:**
- QUICK CUTS:
  - Dancing in a neon-lit room
  - Cooking breakfast (burnt toast)
  - Fighting in an alley
  - Making up in the rain

**LYRICS:**
"But your love / Is a flickering flame / Not the kind / That carves a name..."

**ACTION:**
- Beautiful. Messy. Real.
- Then: He leaves.
- She's alone again.

---

### SCENE 5: THE KNIFE (3:00-3:45)

**VISUAL:**
- Her apartment. Night.
- She holds a KNIFE.
- Staring at it.

**LYRICS:**
"Still maybe that's what I need / Someone to lay down beside me / Even if it's not real..."

**ACTION:**
- She traces the blade.
- Sets it down.
- Goes to the window.

---

### SCENE 6: THE MORNING (3:45-4:30)

**VISUAL:**
- Sunrise. City wakes up.
- She's still at the window.
- The knife is gone.

**LYRICS:**
"Morning breaks / Street lights die / The sun reveals / What we deny..."

**ACTION:**
- She gets dressed.
- Leaves the apartment.
- Walks into the city.

---

### SCENE 7: CODA (4:30-4:55)

**VISUAL:**
- Same alleyway. Same neon sign.
- She passes.
- Doesn't stop.

**LYRICS:**
*(whispered)* "You're the story of my knife..."

**RONSTRAPP BAND:**
- Playing in a lit window above.
- She looks up. Nods.
- Walks on.

---

## MUSIC VIDEO DIRECTOR'S NOTES

- **Vibe:** Neo-noir romance, rain-soaked melancholy, dangerous attraction
- **Key Imagery:** Rain, neon signs, knife, alleyway, sunrise
- **Color:** Blue neon, gray rain, red blood/heart, silver knife
- **Symbolism:** 
  - Knife = dangerous love, potential for harm
  - Rain = perpetual sadness
  - Neon = fleeting attractions
  - Sunrise = new day, letting go
- **Story Arc:** Waiting → Meeting → Relationship → Loss → Confrontation → Release → Moving On
