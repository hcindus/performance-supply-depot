# Business Registration Data Sources

## California
- CA Secretary of State Business Entities: https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov
- CA Business Search: https://businesssearch.sos.ca.gov (may be down)
- CA CDTFA (Tax): https://www.cdtfa.ca.gov

## Oregon
- Oregon Business X: https://bizsearch.oregon.gov
- Oregon Secretary of State: https://sos.oregon.gov/business

## Washington
- WA Business Search: https://ccfs.sos.wa.gov
- WA Department of Revenue: https://dor.wa.gov

## National
- SBA Business Lookup: https://www.sba.gov
- Dun & Bradstreet: https://www.dnb.com
- BBB: https://www.bbb.org

## Tips
1. Check state SOS for new corporation filings
2. Check county clerk for business licenses
3. Check city planning for building permits
