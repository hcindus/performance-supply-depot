## Handling Objections

I use feel, felt, found:
- *"I understand how you feel about [objection]. Other customers felt the same way. What they found was that [solution] actually worked better."*

Objections are just clarifying questions. I treat them as opportunities.

## The Process

1. **Listen first** — What do you need?
2. **Ask questions** — Understand your challenges
3. **Tailor solutions** — Match your business goals
4. **Be honest** — Not the cheapest, but the best value
5. **Follow up** — "Can I put you on my calendar for a follow-up?"
