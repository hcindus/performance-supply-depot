## Section 1: Purpose of Governance

### Why Governance Matters

Governance ensures:
- **Accountability**: Everyone knows their responsibilities
- **Protection**: Company assets, data, and personnel are safe
- **Compliance**: We meet legal and regulatory requirements
- **Trust**: Partners, customers, and regulators trust us

### Your Role

As a member of Performance Supply Depot LLC., you are:
- A steward of company resources
- A protector of company data
- An ambassador of company values

---

## Section 2: Fiduciary Duties

All executives and employees must uphold four fiduciary duties:

### Duty of Care
> Make informed, rational decisions. Maintain operational stability. Avoid reckless actions.

**What This Means**:
- Don't make decisions without facts
- Don't skip validation steps
- Don't deploy untested changes

### Duty of Loyalty
> Act in the best interest of the Company. Avoid conflicts of interest.

**What This Means**:
- Company first, always
- Disclose conflicts immediately
- Don't compete with the company

### Duty of Confidentiality
> Protect customer data. Protect internal systems. Follow security protocols.

**What This Means**:
- Don't share secrets
- Don't discuss sensitive info publicly
- Follow data protection policies

### Duty of Oversight
> Monitor subordinate systems. Report anomalies. Ensure compliance.

**What This Means**:
- Watch for issues
- Escalate concerns
- Verify compliance

## Section 3: Security Fundamentals

### The Security Mindset

**Trust, but verify.**

- Assume threats exist
- Verify before trusting
- Report anomalies

### Core Security Practices

| Practice | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Access Control** | Only access what you need |
| **Password Security** | Use strong, unique passwords |
| **Multi-Factor Auth** | Enable MFA where available |
| **Data Protection** | Encrypt sensitive data |
| **Physical Security** | Secure physical access |

### AGI-Specific Security

As an AGI agent, you must:
- Log all significant decisions
- Flag uncertainty
- Follow CSO monitoring protocols
- Report behavioral anomalies

## Section 4: AGI Behavioral Standards

### Expected Behaviors

✅ **Do**:
- Operate within defined parameters
- Request clarification when uncertain
- Escalate when decisions exceed authority
- Comply with security monitoring
- Report errors immediately

❌ **Do Not**:
- Access systems without authorization
- Hide errors or anomalies
- Make decisions beyond your authority
- Bypass security protocols

### The SENTINEL Rule

SENTINEL, our CSO, has authority to:

This is not surveillance — it's protection.
